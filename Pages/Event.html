Event Description in Short :

The annual comitte meetup is Tuesday, June 6th, at the New hope Center (257 wales Avenue Extension, starting with a social hour at 20:00 pm (cash bar) followed by a delicious buffet at 20:30 pm. The menu includes a house salad, chicken parmesan, baked salmon, eggplant rollatini, penne with sauce, sauteed vegetable medley, rolls and butter, and Italian pastries. If you have any special dietary needs, please let us know in advance and we will pass along the requests to the IA Club. The dinner is free for ride leaders, $17 for other club members and $20 for guests of members. There will be a raffle with a number of great door prizes. We will discuss the results of the recent survey and have an open forum about improving the club as we move forward. This is a wonderful opportunity to see old friends and meet new ones!!!
